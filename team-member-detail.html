Personal Info

A visa consultant is a service professional who ensures the application process is smooth and hassle-free. They perform a range of duties, including document verification, submission assistance, and compliance checks.

The Visa Processing Team Leader leads a team of consultants in handling visa applications. They ensure applications are processed according to company and immigration standards. They respond to client inquiries and specific requests, communicating visa requirements to the team. They train new staff on application procedures and the use of processing tools. They ensure all documentation is accurate and compliant. They inform supervisors or managers when additional resources or updates to visa policies are needed.
